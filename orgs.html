Hunter R. Rawlings III Cornell Presidential Research Scholars

Website ⇱
  • Rawlings Scholars are Undergraduate students who were selected because they have shown interest in research in high school or during their first two years at Cornell.
  • RCPRS supports their scholars by providing a $8000 support account ($5000 if admitted as juniors) to help fund expenses while pursuing their research
  • This money can be used for pay if research is done for wage or to help fund living and travel expenses if needed.
  • In addition to the expectation of research every semester (excluding first semester of freshman year), scholars are expected to complete 1 summer of full-time research.
  • Scholars choose the faculty mentor they want to work under join projects they have a passion to pursue.
Source: RCPRS FB Page

Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers

Main Website ⇱ Cornell Website ⇱
  • SASE is a nation-wide organization to help Asian scientists and engineers reach their full potential.
  • It aids in professional development, education and employment for its members.
  • At Cornell, SASE hosts events too boost member engagement on campus.
  • There are also panels where professionals in different industries come and talk to the members and provide insight and advice for working in the global business world.
  • Every year, a few members are selected to go attend the SASE Nation Conference with members from schools all around the country attend.
Source: SASE

Phi Sigma Pi National Honor Fraternity Beta Nu Chapter

Main Website ⇱ Cornell Website ⇱
  • Phi Sigma Pi is built on a tripod of ideals, Scholarship, Leadership, and Fellowship
  • As a Brother, you have to maintain a 3.0 or higher GPA and have good academic standing
  • PSP helps students gain leadership skills and teaches them how to apply and use them in their communities. PSP Leadership also emphasizes philanthropy
  • PSP promotes Fellowship by fostering a sense of teamwork that allows for successful planning of events and activities. The friendly culture allows for meaningful life-long relationships to be built between the Members.
  • As an Initiate, I attend weekly initiate meetings and prepare to be inducted as a Brother.
Source: Phi Sigma Pi
